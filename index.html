Privacy Policy

At Cozke Enterprise, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers, in full compliance with Amazon’s Data Protection Policies (DPP) and applicable regional regulations. This policy describes how we collect, use, store, protect, share, and delete your information.

Data We Collect

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number) provided by you.
  • Marketplace seller account details required for account management.
  • Information needed to provide our services, such as product data, campaign performance, sales data (only as necessary).

Use of Data

  • To deliver, optimize and report on account management services for your selected platforms.
  • To provide support and communicate with you.
  • No personal data is sold or used for any third-party marketing purposes.

Data Storage & Protection

  • All collected data is stored securely following industry standards and Amazon’s DPP requirements.
  • Only authorized personnel can access client data for service delivery.

Data Sharing

  • We may share information with marketplace platforms, but only as required for account operations or compliance.
  • No data is shared with external parties unless required by law or by explicit client instruction.

Data Deletion

  • Upon request or at the end of the service relationship, your account data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal/compliance reasons.

Compliance

  • Cozke Enterprise complies with Amazon’s Data Protection Policies (DPP), Indian IT Act, and any other applicable regulations.
  • Clients are encouraged to review both Amazon’s and local laws applicable to seller account data.

Contact for Privacy Matters

For privacy inquiries, data access, or deletion requests, please email: cozke.ecom@gmail.com
